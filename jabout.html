ABOUT

Tic-Tik is a platform which helps to have a track of the time of different cities of different countries belonging to different continents carrying different time zones. Adding to that, there is also live tracking of weather with options to view it in different units.

The bad news is time flies and weather changes. The good news is you are the pilot, feeling it.
